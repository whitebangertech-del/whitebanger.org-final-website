Why Startup India Recognition Changes Everything

When the Government of India recognizes a company under Startup India, it's a seal of legitimacy and opportunity. It means tax exemptions, access to the ₹10,000 crore Fund of Funds, fast-track patent applications, and direct participation in government procurement programs.

For White Banger students, our recognition means you can leverage our status to validate your own ventures — and you'll learn from an institution that has walked the entrepreneurship path itself.
