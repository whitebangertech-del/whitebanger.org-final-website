ABOUT THE INITIATIVE

Born from the Vision of Shri Naveen Jindal

Naveen Avsar International Skill Centre, Kaithal (नवीन अवसर योजना, "New Opportunities") was conceived by Shri Naveen Jindal, the sitting Member of Parliament from Kurukshetra, with a singular purpose: to ensure that every young person in Kaithal, regardless of economic background, has equal access to professional, skill-based education.

Recognising that digital skills are the foundation of modern employment, Shri Naveen Jindal chose White Banger, a nationally recognised and industry-accredited platform, as his preferred technology partner. In collaboration with RKSD College & RVS Society, this initiative brings certified courses in Digital Marketing, AI & Automation, Web Development, Graphic Design, and more, directly to the doorstep of Kaithal's youth.

This is not merely an educational programme. It is a movement, a declaration that geography is no longer a barrier to ambition.

SIMPLE 4-STEP PROCESS

How to Join the Skill Centre

Getting started takes just minutes. Here is how eligible students from Kaithal can apply.

1

Fill Enquiry Form

Submit your details via the White Banger enquiry form or visit RKSD College directly. Mention "Naveen Avsar International Skill Centre" in the message.

2

Free Counselling

Attend a free counselling session at RKSD College or on WhatsApp. Our counsellors help you choose the right course for your career goals.

3

Enrol & Get Access

Complete enrolment at RKSD College and receive immediate access to White Banger's digital learning platform and batch schedule.

4

Learn, Certify & Get Placed

Complete your course, collect your dual certification, and benefit from 100% placement support. Your new career starts here.
