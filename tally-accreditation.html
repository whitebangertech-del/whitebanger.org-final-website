What You'll Master
in Our Tally Program

📊

Core Accounting

  • Chart of accounts and ledger creation
  • Journal entries, debit/credit notes
  • Bank reconciliation statements
  • Trial balance and financial statements
  • Multi-currency transactions

GST Compliance

  • GST setup and configuration in Tally
  • GSTR-1, GSTR-2, GSTR-3B filing

  • TDS returns and challans
