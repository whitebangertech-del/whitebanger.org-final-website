NIELIT Accreditation

What is NIELIT?

NIELIT (National Institute of Electronics and Information Technology) is a government organization under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY). It is India's premier institute for creating IT talent and promoting IT literacy nationwide.

White Banger's NIELIT accreditation demonstrates our adherence to government-approved standards and commitment to quality IT and digital skill education for all Indians.
