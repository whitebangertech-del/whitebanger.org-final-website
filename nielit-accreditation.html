About NIELIT

A Government-Backed Credential That Opens Doors

NIELIT (formerly DOEACC) was established by the Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology to promote IT literacy across India. Our NIELIT approval means every certificate we issue is officially recognized for government job applications, promotions, and private sector hiring alike.

From entry-level computer courses to advanced IT specializations — NIELIT's tiered certification system (O, A, B, C levels) gives your career a clear progression path.

🏛️

Valid for Government Jobs

NIELIT certificates are accepted as proof of IT qualification in government recruitment processes nationwide.

📋

Nationally Standardized Exams

Uniform examination and evaluation process ensures your certificate has consistent value anywhere in India.

🎓

University Credit Transfer

Many universities recognize NIELIT levels as equivalent to academic IT subjects — saving you time and money.
